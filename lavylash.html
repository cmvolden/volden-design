Outline

This client wanted to refresh her small business' existing logo and have a website created. She caters to some high end clientele, so she wants to present herself professionally while maintaining a modern feel.

Logo Process

The client came to me with a rough logo she had created using Canva, and wanted it to be recreated using similar but more versatile and global fonts.

I selected Stilson Display Condensed as the serif font and Poynter Gothic Text Condensed as the sans-serif. Unfortunately since the creation of the logo in 2019, both fonts have been removed from Adobe's library, so it will be revisited.

Website Process

The goals of the website were to provide an official and legitimate website to clients rather than relying on her Facebook page, to have a link to her existing booking service, to showcase her work and certifications, and list her services.

The client has been very casual with the completion date of the website due to changes in her business caused by COVID-19, so it has been a work in progress for some time. We are continually adding to it and editing content as her business needs change with a final product in sight for 2021.
