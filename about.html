Hi, I’m Christi!

I didn’t know I wanted to be a Graphic Designer until I was seated in class and learning. But once I realized — I knew there was nothing else I wanted to become. I’m driven by the ability and determination to create change through purposeful design, whether it be for accessibility, to benefit the environment, or to help a small business reach its full potential.

Previous experience in Psychology and Retail Management has developed my communication skills, and my previous employers have always appreciated my efficiency and resourcefulness. Courteous and empathetic with a good sense of humour, I’m the quiet observer that people come to for a straightforward opinion.

I'm passionate about all things design, and am happiest when I can do a combination of Print, Web, and UI/UX. I love to learn and am always happy to take on a challenge or a task outside of my comfort zone.

I enjoy meeting likeminded people so feel free to reach out regarding a potential project or position, or just to chat!
