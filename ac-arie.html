Outline

During the summer of 2020 I worked on Phase II of a collaboration project between Algonquin's Applied Research, Innovation & Entrepreneurship (ARIE) department, the Algonquin Library, OCE, and Adobe. The team was comprised of a Professor within the School of Business, and program graduates from Project Management, Photography, Human-Centered Design, Advertising & Design, and Graphic Design.

The goal of the project was to increase use of Adobe subscriptions within the college, while increasing student's employability by helping them create portfolios of their work using Adobe Spark.

Die Lines

Using Illustrator, I created die lines for the new package which would fit on a single sheet of letter sized paper using only a single glue line, and fold lines for the rest. An optional clear sticker on the top and bottom of the package may be used to prevent the package being opened in-store.

Typography

By carefully selecting a new font family, I was able to include all of the same information, while making the package more readable, and decrease the overall width of the backing.
