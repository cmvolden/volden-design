"K"APSULE Coffee Filter Packaging Update Proposal

November 3rd, 2020

I am very excited to announce my upcoming personal project: a package redesign for a reusable coffee filter which is intended to be used in Keurigs. The product sold by Today's Housewares was found at Loblaws.

I purchased this item roughly a year ago because I have owned a Keurig for years and the K-Cup waste was really bothering me. I didn't want to buy a whole new coffee machine when mine worked perfectly fine, so I sought out a reusable alternative.

Upon finding the product in store, I was immediately struck with a sense of conflict. Here I was trying to reduce the amount of plastic waste I am responsible for, yet the thing that will do that is packaged entirely in an unnecessary amount of it! I thought that this was a very poor design decision from a company selling an eco-friendly product until I realized that that wasn't what they were selling at all. They were selling a product for convenience to replace a product that is already at its peak level of convenience. Through some rebranding of the product, Today's Housewares will be able to better market this product as an eco-friendly K-Cup alternative.
