Print, Web, UI, UX... you got it.

I’m Christi — A Graphic Designer from Ottawa, Canada with a passion for eco-friendly packaging, web design, UI, UX, and elevating ideas through meaningful design.

Print

Who actually likes creating die lines? ... I do! Designing eco-friendly packaging is one of my greatest passions and something I will continue to focus on throughout my career whether it be professionally or for personal projects.

Check out some of my packaging and non-packaging print projects!

Web

I'm constantly improving my web development skills, and love designing with accessibility and UI/UX in mind. It should never be a struggle to find what you're looking for on a website.

I built this website from scratch, and am continually updating it.

UI & UX

UI and UX are areas of design that I previously thought I wouldn't be very interested in pursuing, but was I ever wrong! I have found myself becoming increasingly conscious of interactions online and out in the wild, appreciating the little things, and striving to improve the friction points! I can't imagine design without considering the UI and UX.
