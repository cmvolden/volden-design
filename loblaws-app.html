Loblaws App Concept

Map My Shop

Type
UI/UX, Group project
Role
In this project I took on a leadership role. I recorded the interviewees, created informational charts within the reports by breaking down the interviews, and created a majority of the interactive prototype.
Tools
Adobe XD
Date
2019

Goal

To create an app concept for Loblaws that utilized both 5G and XR to be used on Android.

Overview

Research told us that people dislike grocery shopping due to long lines, crowds, items being out of stock, and being unsure of item locations. However, these interviewees generally do not trust a shopping service to select their produce and other items. Most interviewees said they would pay for an app which improved their experience.

Our solution was to create an app that would make the process a little easier. Users are able to create a shopping list inside the app from home, utilizing 5G for real-time inventory levels. The app creates the ideal shopping route from the list of items to save the user's time, and uses their location while using the app to determine where they currently are located on their route. Augmented reality allows the shopper to point their camera down an aisle and receive directions to an item (hello tiny jar of pesto on the top shelf).

Further allowing for customization of the app allows the user to select and save a preferred store, or change stores based on their current location which helps those shopping in an unfamiliar city.

Process

By following the Design Sprint process, we brainstormed, interviewed a sample group, created reports and an interactive prototype, observed our sample group using the prototype, and finally presented our findings.

Research

We brainstormed and came up with a basic app concept to help reduce friction points during grocery shopping. After developing this concept, we interviewed a sample group of peers and documented our findings in a research report.

Prototype & User Testing

Once we digested the findings from our research, we created flowcharts, a paper prototype, and an interactive prototype of the app (using Adobe XD) which included a main login screen, shopping list options, a route planner, and an AR aisle view. We had our sample group test the prototype and documented the findings.

Case Study

Our final report covered our findings from start to finish

Presenting our results

As a final step, we were to present the results of our research and prototype to our peers. Due to COVID-19 we ultimately ended up recording narration for the slides.
